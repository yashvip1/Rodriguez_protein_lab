Getting to Yale School of Medicine

Yale School of Medicine is located in the Cedar Street area of New Haven. It is accessible via I-95 (Exit 47) or I-91 (Exit 3), and is served by CT Transit local buses. The nearest train station is Union Station, about 1.5 miles away with taxi/rideshare access.
