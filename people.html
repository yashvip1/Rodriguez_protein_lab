Principal Investigator

Prof. María Rodríguez Martínez

Associate Professor · Dept. of Biomedical Informatics & Data Science · Yale School of Medicine

Prof. Rodríguez Martínez earned her PhD in physics at the Institut d'Astrophysique de Paris. She completed postdoctoral training at the Weizmann Institute of Science (Israel) and Columbia University (USA), then spent over a decade at IBM Research Europe (Switzerland) as Technical Leader of Systems Biology. She joined Yale School of Medicine in May 2024.

Her research bridges state-of-the-art deep learning — transformers, structural predictors, generative AI — with biophysics and computational immunology to decode how immune receptors function in cancer and autoimmunity. She also co-leads the Center for Systems and Engineering Immunology (CSEI) activities at Yale.

Education: PhD Physics, Institut d'Astrophysique de Paris  ·  Postdoc, Weizmann Institute & Columbia University
